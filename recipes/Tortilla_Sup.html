Tortilla Soup

Description

This is a delicious and healthy recipe to prepare when you want to eat something warm and easy to digest.

My sister told me an easy way to prepare it, altough it may not look the same as the image it will taste really good

Ingredients

  • 9 Roma tomatoes
  • 500g chicken breast
  • Half red onion
  • 2 Chipotle spoons
  • Avocado
  • Lime
  • Corn chips

Steps

  1. Boil chicken breast in as little water as possible
  2. Shred chiken
  3. Cut tomatoes in 4 parts, cut red onion and boil for a minute using same water
  4. Add chipotle and blend
  5. Mix chicken and blend
  6. Pour in a bowl
  7. add sliced avocado, corn chips and lime
